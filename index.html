The formula this app uses to compute Bohr's radius is the following:

Bohr's Radius = (n^2 x h^2 x ε0) / (π Me Z e^2)

where n = Principal Quantum Number,
h = Planck's Constant,
ε0 = Permitivity of Free Space,
Me = Mass of Electron,
Z = Atomic Number,
e = Charge on an electron
